Alberta Wilderness Association

Defending Alberta's Wilderness

The Alberta Wilderness Association (AWA) is a charitable society founded in 1965. It's primary objective is the defence of the wilderness in Alberta. AWA works in hand with the local communities to raise awareness about the need for care for Alberta wild spaces and to take necessary actions for the protection of the wilderness. AWA is headquatered in Calgary Alberta and has members, supporters and sponsors both in Alberta and beyond.
